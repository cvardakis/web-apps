About the UDCA

The Utah Debate Coaches Association provides all debate coaches, regardless of type of debate, region, or pedagogical style, with avenues for professional development and support. The goal of the organization is to strengthen the ability of coaches to provide meaningful learning experiences for their debaters. Our ultimate vision is to provide opportunities for high school coaches to strengthen their abilities in order to provide stronger learning environments for their students.
